Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Kala application.

Information Collection and Use

Kala does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera – used to allow users to take photos for their events.
  • Photos / Media – used to allow users to select images from their device gallery.
  • Notifications – used to send reminders and countdown alerts.

How Data is Used

All photos and event data are stored locally on the user's device. Kala does not upload or share this data with any external servers.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data stored on the device.

Third-party Services

Kala does not use third-party services that collect personal data.

Children’s Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
